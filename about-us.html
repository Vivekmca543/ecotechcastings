About Us

Ecotech Castings India Private limited, started in the year 2003 in Coimbatore, Tamilnadu and is a sister concern of Peekay Group (Peekay Steel Castings Pvt. Ltd., Calicut, Kerala) which was established in the year 1942

Our focus is to create market/supply of all precision components in castings, forgings, fabrication and internals in different material grades to all kinds of global valve manufacturers at competitive price and quality.

A team consists of highly qualified and experienced engineers are looking after the designs, quality and vendor development activities and are also well versed to cater the requirements of valve manufacturers especially in Europe and other countries as well.

DIRECTORS:

1. K.E. MOIDU - MANAGING DIRECTOR

K.E. Moidu is the Managing Director of Ecotech Castings India Private Limited, Coimbatore. He is also the Managing Director of Peekay Steel Castings (P) Ltd., Calicut and its group Companies. He is a Science Graduate with Post graduate Diploma in Milling Technology. He is an instrumental for Peekay Group’s diversification and entry into steel sector. It was under his leadership, Peekay Steel Castings (P) Ltd., commenced production of steel castings and developed a good clientele for the steel castings in India and abroad.

2. THOUFEEQ AHAMED MOIDU - DIRECTOR

Thoufeeq Ahamed Moidu is a Director of Ecotech Castings India Private Limited, Coimbtaore. He was born in an affluent and noble business family of North Kerala as son of Mr. K.E. Moidu, Managing Director. He graduated as BE in Mechanical Engineering in 2011 from Cardiff University, U.K. He did his Masters in Material Science & Metallurgy, from Sheffield in 2016. He has worked with Sevem Glucon & AST SPA, Italy as a part of his masters’s program. He started his career in Peekay Steel Castings (P) Ltd., Coimbatore as a Management Trainee in 2016 and elevated as Executive Director in 2019
